About Our Company

In October 2023, Ashwin Gajan established Ashwin Gajan T/A Just Parts as a sole proprietorship, succeeding Just Parts cc. We are a supplier to the heavy-duty American truck parts industry, specializing in Cummins engine components. Our inventory also includes parts for International (Navistar) trucks, encompassing chassis and suspension components.

We stock Cummins engine components for Big Cam, Small Cam, NH-Series, B-Series, C-Series, N14, ISB, ISM, ISX, L10, M11 engines. We cater for brands as Automann, FP Diesel, Interstate McBee, PAI and ensuring the supply of only premium quality parts.

Our commitment is to consistently provide our clients with the most efficient and highest level of service.
